Listn

Mobile App

12 / 2012

Role: Cofounder, designer

Listn is a platform for seamlessly sharing music with your friends. Whether you have mp3s or use streaming services like Spotify or Rdio, Listn lets you connect and share across all major platforms.

I cofounded Listn with four friends in May 2012. As the team's designer, I was in charge of UX, UI, user studies, branding, and front-end implementation in Objective-C.

In August we were accepted into FounderFuel, where I had the chance to work alongside brilliant designers from Jet Cooper. I left the team in December to finish up my last semester of school in Kingston, but the team continues to operate out of RocketSpace in California.
