Background

I'm not in the habit of bringing business cards around with me but in March I attended a conference that required them for entry. I'm pretty easy to find online, as my username never changes and it's my actual name to boot. I used this to my advantage by merging the standard name, email, website, and twitter handle lines into what you see above.

Since the main purpose of a business card is to concisely convey contact information, I was nervous that this approach would confuse some recipients. I ran some informal user tests before printing large quantities and had a great response.
